About The Event

This graVITas’20, IET-VIT and CSED-VIT collaborate to provide the best platform to nurture and promote tomorrow's potential change-makers within the student community to showcase their solution to society’s most crucial problems.

Presenting “Social Transformers”, the greatest opportunity for all the astonishingly agile brains waiting for the perfect platform, to present their phenomenal ideas on technology, entrepreneurship and innovation.

As the world progresses, we are faced with new and existing challenges to our society. To face these problems head-on, the stage is set for you, the future innovators, to bring your best foot forward because we believe that even the smallest ideas can lead to path-breaking innovation.

Where

Downtown Conference Center, New York

When

Monday to Wednesday
10-12 December

GRAVITAS

Thecnical Fest

The annual techno-management knowledge carnival of VIT, graVITas attracts students from India and Abroad and provides a platform to display their spectacular talents and innovations, aiming to hone the technological skills of the students and researchers with innovative events in every avenue of engineering. This year, graVITas aims to achieve new heights as one of, if not the biggest Virtual Mega Event for the student community.

Follow Gravitas —

IET-VIT

technical chapter

Being one of the world's leading organisations in technical education and thought, the Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET), based in London, has made it its goal to bring technology to the masses through technical education and awareness, help direct those in positions of power on a path of inclusive innovation, create new leaders in the world of Tech and work on solving technical problems that matter.

Follow IET-VIT —

CEDS

organisation

Center for Social Entrepreneurship and Development is an organization which provides a platform for young entrepreneurs to make their startup ideas into a reality by providing an appropriate environment for their successful growth. We help the young generation of entrepreneurs understand the ins and outs of the startup world.
